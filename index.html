Kooth & Qwell

Both posters explore themes of emotion, honesty, and support while reflecting the visual messages often seen in services like Kooth and Qwell. The first poster uses a darker, split-face design to show inner conflict and the weight of dishonesty, while the second uses bright collage textures and repeated words like “HELP,” “HOPE,” and “WORTH” to express the importance of speaking up. Together, they show different emotional experiences while connecting to the supportive and encouraging tone associated with Kooth and Qwell.

Takeaway Fridays & Treat Box

These two logo designs were created to visually express the themes behind “Takeaway Fridays” and the “Treat Box,” using bold illustration and playful colour choices to suit each idea. The Takeaway Fridays logo features a friendly chef and flowing handwritten text to create a warm, food-focused identity, while the Treat Box logo uses a donut, utensils, and star elements for a fun, energetic feel that highlights enjoyment and reward. The added phrase “You Deserve One” strengthens the message by making it more personal and encouraging, reinforcing the idea that everyone deserves a small treat at the end of the week.

NHS

This project focuses on raising awareness about the correct handling, maintaince, and safety standards of NHS medical equipment. The video explains why keeping devices updated, serviced, and properly calibrated is crucial for preventing failures, protecting patients, and supporting clinical satff. It highlights the risks of outdated or poorly maintained equipment and reinforces the imporantce of routine checks, clear documentation, and following offical NHS safety protocols.

Wise

Wise is a money-transfer and multi-currency app that makes it easy to send, spend, and save across borders with low fees and real exchange rates. It gives users a clear, convenient way to manage everyday finances—whether they’re sending money abroad, holding multiple currencies, or tracking spending on the Wise card. I redesigned the app to make these core features even easier to access and understand. The goal was to create a smoother, more intuitive experience that supports Wise’s purpose : helping people move and manage their money confidently, without confusion or hidden costs.

Wild Fell

This poster, designed to bring the dialogue from Wild Fell Extract- Landscape of Flowers is a Landscape of Hope-to life, It features a vivid, immersive landscape where lush of green mountains meet a cascading waterfall, flowing into a serene sea under a moonlit blue sky. The foreground shows a lone figure standing, reflecting admist the natural beauty, symbolizing contemplation and optimism. Flowers dot the scene, adding burts of color that reinforce the theme of hope and renewal. Every element, from the flowing water to the vast sky, is crafted to evoke sereinty, inspiration, and the enduring beauty of nature.
